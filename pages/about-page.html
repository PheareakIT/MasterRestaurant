About our Dishes

At our restaurant, every dish is crafted to celebrate the unique flavors of Khmer cuisine. We offer a variety of traditional Cambodian dishes that combine fresh herbs, homemade spices, and authentic cooking techniques passed down through generations

From the refreshing Somlor Machu (sour soup) and creamy Amok Trey (fish amok) to the rich flavors of Khmer red curry, each plate is cooked with care to bring out true Cambodian taste. Our grilled meats, stir-fried noodles, and seasonal specialties are prepared using local ingredients to keep every bite fresh and delicious.

Whether you're craving something spicy, savory, or comfortingly traditional, our dishes are made to satisfy and bring you the real essence of Khmer food.

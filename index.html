Master Battambang

Master serves Living Cambodian Cuisine in a tranquil courtyard with flowing fish pond, greeneries & intricate architecture surrounded by indoor seating. Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, Master also offers private dining rooms for business lunches or special events.

MASTER RESTAURANT

Master is a high-end Cambodian restaurant that prides itself on offering Living Cambodian Cuisine. Through dedicated research, practice, and promotion of Cambodian culinary traditions, Master strives to preserve the nation’s rich food heritage for generations to come.

Master Cambodian Cuisine draws inspiration from the ancient Angkor period, offering a unique flavor profile created through a masterful blend of spices. Each dish reflects Cambodia’s rich botanical heritage.

Our Master Chef

Our chef is a proud Cambodian culinary expert with a deep passion for preserving and sharing the authentic flavors of Khmer cuisine. With years of experience in traditional Cambodian cooking, our chef combines the wisdom of old family recipes with modern techniques to create dishes that are both authentic and beautifully presented.

Every meal is prepared with dedication, using fresh local ingredients and carefully balanced spices to bring out the true essence of Cambodian flavor. Our chef believes that food is not just something to eat—it is a story of culture, tradition, and love.

Thanks to our chef’s talent and creativity, each dish on our menu delivers the taste of home while offering a memorable dining experience for every guest.

About our Dishes

At our restaurant, every dish is crafted to celebrate the unique flavors of Khmer cuisine. We offer a variety of traditional Cambodian dishes that combine fresh herbs, homemade spices, and authentic cooking techniques passed down through generations
